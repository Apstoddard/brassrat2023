Thank you for your interest in joining the Class of 2024 Ring Committee. Answer the questions linked in the application and read the instructions CAREFULLY. But first, let us tell you about who we are and what we do!

If you ask any Ring Comm alum to talk about their time on the committee, they will say it was their most rewarding experience at MIT. Period. We have the honor and responsibility of creating the Brass Rat for our class, one of the longest standing traditions at MIT. Each twelve-person committee has the ability to redesign every aspect of the ring, defining their class's legacy. But designing the ring is just the beginning. We also plan Ring Premiere and Ring Delivery, highly anticipated class-wide events. We interact with Herff Jones, our partner company, MIT leadership, and other stakeholders over the year on the committee. Being on this committee is one of the most rewarding things that any of us have ever done, but it is a lot of work and responsibility. We meet year-round for up to 5 hours a week (formally), but that number is subject to change/increase as we get closer to momentous events like Premiere and Delivery!

But we haven't even mentioned the best part: the people. Ring Committee is composed of students from every corner of campus, and all twelve of us come together to make the ring that our class deserves. After spending so much time together, we become a family that loves and supports each other for the rest of our time here at MIT. Also, all members of RingComm are very fortunate to receive up to a 10K gold ring free of charge!!

Watch the video above to get an even better sense of what it's like being on Ring Committee--we hope you fall in love with it as much as we have!

Much love ❤,
RingComm '23
